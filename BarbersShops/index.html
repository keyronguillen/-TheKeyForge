Our Story

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Craft in Malta

Founded in 2015 on Tower Road, Sliema, Blade & Stone was born from a single belief: every man deserves a cut that commands respect. What began as a two-chair shop has grown into one of Malta's most trusted grooming destinations — a place where the old-world art of barbering meets contemporary style.

Whether you're a Maltese local coming in for your weekly trim, a Valletta professional needing a sharp look before a board meeting, or an expat discovering the island — you'll find our chairs ready, our conversation warm, and our blades sharper than ever.

  • ◈
    Precision Every Time No rushed cuts. Every line is intentional.
  • ◈
    Premium Products Only American Crew, Layrite & traditional shaving soaps.
  • ◈
    Community First A local institution for Malta's diverse community.
