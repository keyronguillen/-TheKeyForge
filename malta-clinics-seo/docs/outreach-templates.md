## 🎯 The pitch narrative (why a good website + SEO matters — say this on the call)

1. **Patients search before they call.** ~90% check Google/Maps first. If you're not in the
   Map Pack with reviews, you're invisible at the moment of decision.
2. **No booking = lost leads.** Half of booking intent happens after hours when your phone is off.
   An online booking widget captures it 24/7.
3. **Trust is visual.** A clean, fast, secure (HTTPS) mobile site signals a quality clinic.
   A dated/insecure one signals the opposite — before they meet you.
4. **High-ticket treatments justify the spend.** One extra implant or smile-makeover patient a
   month pays for the entire website + SEO programme many times over.
5. **Malta's medical-tourism edge.** English-speaking + EU + lower cost = ranking for
   "dental implants Malta" captures UK/IT patients worth €€€ each.

**The ask:** a 15-min call → free audit → demo landing page (Section 2) → proposal.
