## 2. Most important failures to improve (per clinic)

### 🔴 Common across ALL 5 (highest-impact, fix first)
1. **No structured data / Schema markup** (`LocalBusiness` / `MedicalBusiness` / `Dentist`, `Review`, `FAQPage`).
   → Loses rich snippets, star ratings and Google Maps prominence.
2. **Missing / inconsistent image `alt` text** → accessibility + image-search traffic lost.
3. **Thin or absent local-SEO signals** — no NAP consistency block, no embedded Google Map, no per-location landing pages.

### Clinic-by-clinic

**1. ForteDent**
- No email contact published (phone only) → friction + missed lead capture.
- **No online booking system** — biggest conversion gap.
- Alt text missing on logo/badges; Schema not present.
- "News" section exists but appears empty → no content marketing engine.

**2. Signature Dental** *(strongest of the five)*
- No Schema markup (`MedicalBusiness`, `Review`, hours).
- No Google Map embed / weak location signals.
- Minimal internal linking between service pages and blog.

**3. ProDent Care**
- Meta descriptions not set on key pages.
- No image alt text, no Schema, no breadcrumbs.
- No FAQ schema despite obvious dental Q&A demand.
- Blog exists (30+ posts) but lacks author/date metadata enrichment.

**4. Regional Dental Clinic** *(weakest — quickest wins)*
- **Running on HTTP, not HTTPS** → 🚨 security warning in browsers, patient data + ranking risk. **Fix immediately.**
- No meta descriptions, no Schema.
- **No blog / zero content marketing.**
- No social media links, no email contact.

**5. Dental Implantology Unit Malta**
- Meta title/description not clearly set on homepage.
- Unclear H1 hierarchy; missing image alt text.
- No online booking; only a footer email (no prominent form).
- "News" link present but no real content cadence.

## 4. NOTES — explanation of Steps 1 & 2 (for the client)

**Step 1 — How the 5 clinics were found.**
We searched the live Maltese market (Yellow.com.mt, WhatClinic, welcome-center-malta) for
private dental/aesthetic clinics concentrated in the Sliema–St Julian's–Swieqi corridor.
This area has the highest density of expats, tourists and medical-tourism searches, so SEO
wins there convert into real, high-ticket bookings (implants, cosmetic). We picked 5 real,
comparable, active businesses so the pitch is concrete, not hypothetical.

**Step 2 — How the failures were identified.**
We did a manual front-end audit of each homepage and checked the signals Google actually
ranks on:
- **On-page:** meta title & description, H1 hierarchy, image alt text.
- **Technical:** HTTPS, mobile viewport, structured data (Schema/JSON-LD).
- **Local:** NAP (name/address/phone) consistency, Google Map, social profiles.
- **Conversion:** online booking, visible email/contact form.
- **Content:** presence and freshness of a blog.

The recurring gaps — **no Schema, missing alt text, no/weak online booking, and (for one)
no HTTPS** — are exactly the items that cost rankings *and* lost bookings, which is why they
top the action list.

## 5. Senior Marketing add-on (beyond pure SEO)

Because the brief asks for a 10-yr marketing lens, not just technical SEO:

- **Google Business Profile (GBP) is the #1 lever in Malta** — for "near me" searches the Map
  Pack beats organic. Optimise GBP (photos, services, posts, Q&A, review replies) before
  anything else. Cheap, fast, huge ROI.
- **Reviews = ranking + trust.** Automate review requests by SMS/WhatsApp post-visit. Reply to
  every review (signals freshness to Google).
- **Medical tourism angle.** UK/Italian patients search "dental implants abroad cost". Build
  multilingual landing pages (EN/IT) with transparent pricing — Malta's edge is EU + English-speaking.
- **Content engine.** Topic clusters: "dental implants cost Malta", "Invisalign Malta", "teeth
  whitening Sliema". One pillar + supporting posts per service.
- **Conversion / CRO.** Booking widget above the fold, click-to-call on mobile, WhatsApp chat
  button, exit-intent offer.
- **Paid + retargeting.** Google Ads on high-intent terms + Meta retargeting for the
  consideration-heavy treatments (implants, cosmetic).
- **Measurement.** GA4 + GBP Insights + Search Console; track *calls and bookings*, not just
  traffic. Tie spend to booked revenue.
- **GDPR/compliance.** Malta is EU — cookie consent, privacy policy, lawful basis for marketing
  emails. (Built into the landing page in Section 2.)
